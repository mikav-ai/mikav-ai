## We're in.

Mikav has been selected for **SparkX** — Kerala Startup Mission's Creative & Cultural Pre-Incubation Program. A 45-day intensive designed to bridge cultural heritage and modern entrepreneurship.

## What is SparkX?

SparkX is a pre-incubation initiative by Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) to nurture entrepreneurial talent in the creative and cultural domains.

- Identify talent in performing arts, digital archives, and heritage tech
- Assess market-ready opportunities from Kerala's cultural IP
- Provide startup thinking to convert artistic ideas into viable ventures

## The Curriculum

Six strategic masterclasses:

1. **Introduction to Creative Startups** — Cultural IP as a business
2. **IP & Cultural Ownership** — Copyrights and licensing for traditional art
3. **Design Thinking for Artists** — Artistic concepts into tangible products
4. **Market Validation** — Discovery techniques to find your audience
5. **Revenue & Monetization** — Digital platforms, tourism, creator economy
6. **The Art of the Pitch** — Pitch decks for grants and incubation

## Residency Week

A 7-day intensive offline residency in Kochi and Kalamandalam:

- Founder training — business modeling and soft skills
- Fab Lab orientation — prototyping tools at KSUM Kochi
- Leadership interface — session with the CEO of Kerala Startup Mission

## Why This Matters for Mikav

Mikav bridges Kerala's cultural heritage to real-world venture. SparkX gives us direct access to domain experts, startup mentors, and KSUM's incubation ecosystem.

This validates what we've been building — the bridge between culture knowledge and entrepreneurial action.
